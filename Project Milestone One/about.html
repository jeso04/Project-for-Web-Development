About Me

If this is what I end up not doing, I will probably just open up a local chain serving a mid-sized city like Augusta or Columbus and it's surrounding metropolitan area.

I have been having a great experience so far at Kennesaw State. I have no plan to move to another school as I have been doing well enough here I feel. I have been a great time in this class and hope to finish it very nicely!
